Courses

Semester 1

In semester one, the modules I took were: Programming 1, Introduction to Modelling and Problem Solving, Calculus 1 and Algebra. This semester included topics on both programming and maths, which were a good introduction to the course. In addition, these subjects linked well together, making it easier to understand the content. For example, some of Calculus 1 and Algebra's content was applied in the Introduction to Modelling and Problem Solving module.

Semester 2

Currently, in semester two, I am taking Object-Oriented Programming, Web Development, Combinatorics and Mapping and Monitoring the Environment. I made a slight change this semester by taking a geography and maths module instead of two maths subjects again. This change was due to me wanting to experience a different subject, so that maths doesn't take up too much of my timetable. The geography module introduced me to new topics, such as GIS, which provided some variety compared to studying primarily computing and maths.
